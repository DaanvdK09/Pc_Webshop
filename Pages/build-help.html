Choose Your Use Case

Identify what you primarily want to do with your PC. Are you focused on gaming at high FPS, video editing in 4K, streaming your gameplay, or just daily productivity? Your use case defines the components you’ll need.

  • Gaming: Prioritize powerful GPU and CPU combo.
  • Content Creation: Focus on CPU cores/threads and RAM.
  • Streaming: Balanced CPU/GPU with capture cards or software.
  • Everyday Use: Budget builds with efficiency and reliability.

Pick Your Budget

Define your budget to find the best parts that fit your needs without overspending. We recommend:

  • Budget (Under $800): Great for entry-level gaming and productivity.
  • Midrange ($800 - $1500): Smooth gaming at 1080p/1440p, good multitasking.
  • High-end ($1500 - $3000): Max settings in 1440p/4K, streaming, content creation.
  • Ultra High-end ($3000+): Best of the best, future-proof, extreme workloads.
